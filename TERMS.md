## What This Tool Does

idea-reality-mcp provides algorithmic reality scores based on publicly available
data from GitHub, Hacker News, npm, PyPI, and Stack Overflow.

## Not Professional Advice

Reality scores are **estimates** based on public data. They are NOT professional
market research, investment advice, or business consulting. Scores may change
as public data changes.

You are solely responsible for any business, product, or investment decisions

## Data Collection

- **MCP stdio mode**: No data is collected. Queries stay on your machine.
- **REST API** (mnemox.ai): Anonymous analytics only — hashed IPs, query
  timestamps, and aggregated usage stats. No personal data is stored.
- **Discord webhook**: API queries are logged to a private Discord channel
  for service monitoring. No personal identifiers are included.
